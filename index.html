My dearest,

A letter for the girl who's about to conquer the world.

In a few days you'll be leaving to start a new chapter of your life abroad. I'm genuinely so proud of you. It makes me happy to see you chasing your dreams, even though I'll miss having you around every day. Life is going to change for both of us. We'll get busy with our studies, work, responsibilities, and different time zones. There will be days when we'll barely get time to talk, and there will be nights when we'll miss each other more than words can explain.

But here's one promise I want you to remember forever...

I promise to love you every single day. Every morning, every night, every moment in between. Not just today, not just tomorrow, but every day that comes after. My love for you won't become less because of distance — it will only grow stronger. I'll choose you again and again, every single day.

I know we've been having a few hard days lately. We've argued, we've gotten angry, we've misunderstood each other, and sometimes we've reacted more than we should have. But none of those fights ever changed one thing...

I never stopped caring about you. Not for a second.

No matter how upset I get, my heart still worries about you, still prays for your happiness, still wants to know if you've eaten, if you're smiling, if you're okay. Caring for you isn't something I do because everything is perfect. I do it because I love you, and that's never going to change.

I don't want you to leave carrying sadness because of our silly fights. I want you to leave with a heart full of happiness, knowing that someone here loves you endlessly and is waiting for every call, every message, every "I reached safely," every little update.

And honestly... I hope before you leave, we'll sort out these tiny fights. Because you know what? Fights are just a part of love. They don't define us. What defines us is choosing each other after every disagreement. Love isn't about never fighting — it's about never giving up on each other.

Hehe... and do you know a little secret? I'm making this website for you because somewhere deep inside my heart, I already know our fights will end. I know one day you'll stop being angry, you'll stop reacting to my messages, and everything will become normal again. And when that day comes...

Surpriseeeee! ❤️

I'll show you this website, and hopefully it'll make you smile that cute smile I love so much. Because I don't just care about today. I care about our future. I care about every memory we'll make, every reunion after the distance, every hug that will make up for the days we couldn't be together, every dream we'll build side by side.

Distance can separate two places, but it can never separate two hearts that genuinely want each other.

So go and conquer your dreams, my beautiful girl. I'll be here... loving you, supporting you, believing in you, praying for you, missing you, and choosing you — every single day.

Take care of yourself for me. And whenever you look at the sky, just remember... no matter which country we're in, we're still under the same moon, the same stars, and the same promise.

I love you today. I'll love you tomorrow. And I'll keep loving you a little more with every passing day.

Forever yours ❤️
